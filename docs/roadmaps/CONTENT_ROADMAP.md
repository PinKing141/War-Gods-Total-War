# Content Roadmap

This document is intentionally downstream of the simulation roadmaps.

## Purpose

Use this file to track content expansion only after the core observer simulation is stable enough to generate readable history on its own.

## Read Order

Read this after:

1. `../RULES_FRAMEWORK.md`
2. `MODULARIZATION_ROADMAP.md`
3. `LIVING_CHRONICLE_ROADMAP.md`

## Content Comes After Systems

Do not use this roadmap to justify adding more factions, provinces, events, or scenarios before the following are solid:

- Daily and monthly pulse ordering
- Autonomous faction intent generation
- Logistics and shortage consequences
- Chronicle and summary generation
- Long-run persistence and restart safety

## Future Content Buckets

- Additional kingdoms and starts
- Named dynasties and succession trees
- Regional event packs
- Culture, religion, and legitimacy variants
- Weather and seasonal history packs
- Chronicle voice packs and historian biases

## Rule

If a feature adds more things to simulate instead of improving how the world simulates, it belongs here and should wait until the simulation loop is proven.
