War Gods Total War — custom fantasy world heightmap

Resolution: 3072 x 2048, CK2-style large grand-strategy raster
Format: 16-bit grayscale PNG for terrain generation
Meaning: black = ocean/sea/lowest elevation; white = highest mountain ridges
Design: familiar Afro-Eurasia-inspired layout, but deliberately fantasy-altered with reshaped coasts, inland seas, ring mountains, rifts, extended mountain arcs, and island chains.
Recommended use: import the 16-bit PNG as the macro terrain height layer. Use a separate water mask or treat pure black as sea. Add smaller-scale procedural noise in-engine for local hills/cliffs.
